  The Mt. Trumbull schoolhouse was established in 1922 and used until 1966 by
  the small community of ranchers in the area. The schoolhouse has since been
  restored and is open to the public, unattended. Displays inside tell stories
  of early students and the establishment of the schoolhouse as a bit of a
  community center. But if I may draw your attention to the names on the
  directional signage in the schoolyard...
