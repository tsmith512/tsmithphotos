<Masthead title="West" />

<Story copy="right" filename="Adventures - OAT/DSC_4308.jpg">
  The Oklahoma Adventure Trail is a 1,600ish mile route around Oklahoma hitting
  many historical and interesting sights. It was originally posted to
  a <a href="https://www.advrider.com/f/threads/making-of-the-oklahoma-adventure-trail.836706/">biker forum</a> and
  is mostly unpaved though mostly not difficult. George found it and decided we
  should run it in our cars for New Year's, for kicks.
</Story>

  clockwise to Tulsa over New Year's and came back in the spring for Davis to
  Tulsa via the Ouachitas. In the time between, I made a minor equipment change.
