<Story copy="right" filename="Adventures - Ozarks Road Test/DSC_5469.jpg">
  Evan has been itchin’ to get his Wagovan, a 1986 Honda Civic with a
  “push-button” 4WD system, out on a trip since he got it. So we went for a long
  weekend of light overlanding in the Ozarks in northwest Arkansas. Joining our
  regular posse was Kate in her Jeep Cherokee, and Brady who should have been in
  his Suzuki Samurai but its head gasket and cylinder head cracked, so he joined
  late as a mostly-passenger, but did drag the Scion FR-S on a few dirt roads.
</Story>

<Story copy="left" filename="Adventures - Ozarks Road Test/DSC_4662.jpg">
  Evan also gave me a present, and I think it is the coolest.
</Story>
